Need Help?

If you:

  • Have a question about how to use the Symantec Online Redemption Centre that is not covered in Frequently Asked Questions, OR
  • Have a query about the status of your claim, OR
  • Need to have your product key checked, OR
  • Need to update any of the contact details you entered for your claim before we mail your cheque

You can contact our Norton Promotions Help Desk by email to PacificConsumer@symantec.com.

Emails will be answered within two business days (NSW time).

Please expect a delay in responses.

When contacting the Help Desk please provide the following reference information:

  • your product key
  • your claim confirmation number (if known)
  • the Promotion Code for the offer (from the on-box sticker, or "What is the Promotion Code I need to use?" on the FAQ page)

 

* See Terms & Conditions for full details. The offer is open to Australian and New Zealand residents only. Employees of Symantec and their immediate families, retailers of Norton/Symantec products including retailer staff, agencies or anyone connected with the administration of the promotion are not eligible. Limit of 1 claim per valid full-version product purchased, up to a maximum of 1 claim per person, and 3 claims per household/registered business address. Allow 6-10 weeks for rebate to be processed.
