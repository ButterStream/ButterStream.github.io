How To Claim

Follow the steps below to redeem your appropriate rebate.* Common problems are covered on the Frequently Asked Questions page. If you have a question not covered here, email PacificConsumer@symantec.com for help.

1
Check your subscription status

Make sure you have installed and activated the eligible Norton product on at least one device by registering the 25-digit product key.

The products eligible for this offer are:

Norton Security Premium 2 device retail box/POSA products.
Norton Security Premium 3 device (1+2 year) retail box/POSA products.
Norton Security Premium 5+10 device (1+2 year), and Norton Small Business retail box/POSA products.

Purchased from participating Australian or New Zealand retail stores between November 1st 2016 and February 10, 2107, inclusive.

Note: Activating your product will not automatically register your claim for this offer, you must submit a claim for this offer by following these steps.

Find out how to check the product is activated

2
Find your current Product Key

Your product key is used to validate your eligibility for this promotion. The product key can be found:

From your Norton product packaging

In your new Norton product packaging there is a “Getting Started with Norton” installation card. Your Product Key is located beneath the numbered installation steps. Make sure you download, install and activate the participating product by registering the key to your Norton account before trying to claim this offer.

From your Norton account (if you have discarded the product packaging)

Visit https://manage.norton.com and sign in to your account

3
Click the “Claim Now”button and submit your claim details through the Symantec Online Redemption Centre

  • Enter the promotion code cashback1116 if you purchased Norton Security Premium 2 Device retail box/POSA product, Norton Security Premium 3, 5, or 10 Device (1+2 year) retail box/POSA, or Norton Small Business retail box/POSA products.
  • Select your country of residence from the drop-down
  • Click NEXT

  • Enter your 25-digit product key (5 digits per box, no dashes or spaces)
  • Click NEXT

    (The system will then check your Product Key is for an eligible Norton product activated between the promotion dates.)

    See “Tips on entering your product key” on our FAQ page for detailed instructions.
  • Click NEXT
  • Complete the requested details for your claim
  • Click SUBMIT

Please Note:

Only when you click the SUBMIT button will your claim be registered. You will get a confirmation screen and email with your claim confirmation number and coupon code to claim your eligible rebate. If you don’t get either,confirmation screen or email, see How do I know my claim has been received? for next steps.

CLICK HERE to go to the Symantec Online Redemption Centre and start your claim by following the on-screen instructions.
